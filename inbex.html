/ FAQ

Everything you need to know

Plan a seamless and comfortable stay at Bellavista with answers to our most frequently asked questions.

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in is available from 3:00 PM, and check-out is by 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request and subject to availability.

Is breakfast included?

Yes, a complimentary continental breakfast is included with all room reservations. It is served daily from 7:00 AM to 10:30 AM in our dining area.

Do you offer parking?

We provide complimentary valet parking for all guests. Self-parking options are also available nearby for an additional fee.

Are pets allowed?

We welcome pets up to 25 lbs in our designated pet-friendly rooms for an additional fee of $50 per stay. Please inform us in advance if you plan to bring a pet.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 48 hours or more before check-in receive a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours are subject to a one-night room charge.

Do you have accessible rooms?

Yes, we have several accessible rooms equipped with roll-in showers, grab bars, and wider doorways. Please specify your accessibility needs when booking.
